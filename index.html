About SparkCircuit

SparkCircuit is an emerging Indian electronics company founded by Azad Kumar with a vision to create innovative, reliable, and affordable electronic products for homes, businesses, and industries.

We specialize in designing and developing smart electronic solutions including Bluetooth amplifiers, home theater systems, smart LED controllers, IoT devices, embedded systems, PCB design, and custom electronic products.

Every product we build reflects our commitment to innovation, quality, performance, and customer satisfaction. Our goal is not only to manufacture electronics but also to create technology that simplifies everyday life and inspires future innovation.
